why buy travel money online?

There are so many options on how to spend money when you are abroad; credit cards, ATM's and getting currency from your bank, Post Office or at the airport, but each of these has associated fees and costs and often not much transparency. Once you have compared all the options, buying holiday cash online still comes out the cheapest.

Cash can be used everywhere
and anywhere you go

Whether you are in a coffee shop in Barcelona or a remote village in the South African wine region, travel cash is widely accepted by every shop, hotel, restaurant and transport provider. You simply cannot go wrong having some travel currency on you.

Know what you are spending,
no hidden fees or charges

With cash, what you pay is what you pay, unlike many credit cards that charge a foreign transaction or usage fee when you spend with it abroad. The costs with credit cards vary and are often hidden, but a fee of 3% is not uncommon to use your card abroad and the fees can often be more.

Don't get stung by leaving it
to the last minute

Leaving it until the last minute and getting your travel cash at the Airport costs so much more. Often you can end up paying up to an extra 20%. Airport bureau de change rates are much less competitive and also add on commission or a service charge.

It's so simple, order cash
delivery with a few clicks

Ordering travel money has never been easier. Without leaving the comfort of your own home or office, you can just fill out a quick and easy form and your money could be on its way to you.

Being prepared saves
you money

Getting your money upfront stops you from getting caught by unexpected charges. The typical charge for cash withdrawals from a foreign ATM is 2.5%. In other words, withdraw £500 and the transaction cost will be £12.50 for the withdrawal. In many cases, the banks will not provide you with a good foreign exchange rate either, in which case you may get charged twice.

be ready in advance

Delivery

We use the Royal Mail’s Special Delivery service to send you your foreign currency.

This service is insured for the GBP equivalent of the currency contents. Royal Mail guarantees the next working day after we despatch it to you.

This means that if we despatch it on Tuesday you should receive it on Wednesday. If we despatch on a Friday you would receive it on Monday unless your local service delivers it on Saturday, but this is not guaranteed as a Saturday is not a Royal Mail Special Delivery working day. We do offer additional postage options of delivery before 9am and Saturday delivery for a surcharge, please see additional postage options below.

Please note these important points:

• Deliveries are subject to stock availability and order volumes. We always aim to meet customers dates.

• You should not order today and expect delivery before the date you have requested.

• We advise allowing 2 business days between your travel date and the requested delivery date as a minimum to avoid disappointment.

• When working out when you want your currency to arrive allow for our published working hours.

It is important to us that you are aware of all of the above so that we can transact correctly for you.
