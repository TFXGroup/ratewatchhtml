Best Travel Money Retailer 2025? We’ve Been Nominated – Again! Let’s Go for Gold

April 28, 2025
Share
Share on Facebook LinkedIn Email

Hold onto your passports, because we’ve got some seriously exciting news… Travel FX has been nominated – for the seventh year in a row – for Best Retailer for Foreign Exchange at the British Travel Awards 2025!

Yep, it’s official. We’re once again in the running to be crowned the UK’s favourite travel money provider – and it’s all thanks to YOU, our incredible customers.

Over the years, you’ve supported us with votes, shared our posts, shouted us out to your friends, and shown us more love than we could ever have imagined. You helped us bring home a Bronze in 2024, Silver in 2021, and consistently made sure the big guys knew who we were. And now? It’s time to go for GOLD.

What makes this so special?

Well, here’s the thing: we’re the only online-only travel money retailer in the race. No fancy high-street shops. No huge marketing budgets. Just a small, independent team giving it our all every single day to bring you better rates and brilliant service.

We’re up against some major high-street names – but we know we’ve got something they don’t: the best customers in the business.

So… how do you vote?

Voting opens on 1st May, and we’ll be shouting about it (politely) across our emails and social media. Every single vote helps, and it only takes a minute. As a bonus, everyone who votes is automatically entered into the British Travel Awards’ prize draw – and there are thousands of pounds worth of travel prizes to be won. Nice, right?

We’ll share the link to vote the moment it goes live.

Let’s do this!

We’re proud to be nominated again, and prouder still of the community that’s helped get us here. If you’ve ever exchanged currency with us, recommended us to your mates, or just liked one of our cheeky Instagram posts — this is your win too.

Let’s make 2025 our year. Let’s bring home the gold.
