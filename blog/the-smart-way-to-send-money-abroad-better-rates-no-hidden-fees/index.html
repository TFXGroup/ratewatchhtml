The Smart Way to Send Money Abroad: Better Rates, No Hidden Fees

October 29, 2025
Share
Share on Facebook LinkedIn Email

If you’ve ever sent money abroad through your bank and felt short-changed by the time it arrived, you’re not alone. Between poor exchange rates, transfer charges and sneaky hidden fees, a simple international payment can quickly cost more than you expected.

The good news? There’s a smarter, faster, and far more transparent way to send money overseas — and it starts with Travel FX.

Why banks aren’t always your best option

Many people still use their bank for international transfers out of habit. It feels safe and familiar — but it’s rarely the most cost-effective choice.

Banks often:

  • Apply uncompetitive exchange rates, sometimes 3–5% lower than the market rate

  • Add transfer or handling fees on top

  • Pass on correspondent bank charges, which chip away at the amount that actually arrives

By the time your recipient receives the funds, you’ve effectively paid a premium just for using your bank.

The Travel FX difference

At Travel FX, we think sending money abroad should be simple, secure and great value — with no nasty surprises.

Here’s how we make it smarter:

  • 💷 Better exchange rates – we source rates that beat the high-street banks and big transfer apps

  • 🕒 Fast, efficient transfers – your money arrives quickly and safely

  • 🧾 No hidden fees – the amount you send is the amount your recipient receives

  • 🇬🇧 UK-based and trusted – with over a decade of experience in currency exchange and transfers

Whether you’re helping family back home, paying for a holiday villa, or sending funds for accommodation abroad, Travel FX helps you do it with total confidence.

Transparent pricing, every time

We believe in clear, upfront pricing — no fine print, no surprises. You’ll always see the rate you’re getting and the total cost before confirming your transfer.

That transparency means you can plan your payments properly, knowing exactly how much will arrive at the other side.

Real-world savings

Let’s say you’re sending £5,000 to Europe.
Your bank might give you an exchange rate that’s 3% lower than the market rate — costing you around £150 before any fees are added.

With Travel FX, that’s £150 that stays in your pocket. And over the course of a year, regular payments could save you hundreds.

Send money abroad the smart way

When it comes to sending money overseas, you don’t need to settle for high fees and poor rates.

  • Great exchange rates
