Rate Watch - Frequently asked questions

What is the Travel FX Rate Watch service?

Rate Watch from Travel FX allows you to track a specific exchange rate and receive a notification when your chosen rate is reached.

It’s a simple way to keep an eye on the market without checking rates manually throughout the day.

Is Rate Watch free to use?

Yes, Rate Watch is completely free to use.

You can set up alerts and receive notifications at no cost from Travel FX (standard SMS or data charges may apply depending on your provider).

How do I set up a Rate Watch alert?

Setting up a Rate Watch alert is quick and straightforward:

  1. Choose your product type Select the rate you want to track:
    • Travel money rates
    • Currency buy back rates
    • International transfer rates
  2. Select your currency Choose the currency you want to monitor.
  3. View the rate movement graph You’ll see a graph showing how that currency has performed over the past week, helping you decide your target rate.
  4. Set your target rate Enter the exchange rate you’d like to be alerted for.
  5. Enter your details Provide your name, email address and/or mobile number, and choose how you’d like to be notified (SMS, email, or both).

We’ll then monitor the rate and notify you if your selected rate is reached.

Do I need an account to use Rate Watch?

Yes. When you set up a Rate Watch alert, a Travel FX account is automatically created for you.

This allows you to manage, edit or delete your alerts at any time.

Can I set more than one rate alert?

Yes, you can have up to 5 Rate Watch alerts running at the same time.

Each alert must be set individually, but you can track different currencies, rates or products simultaneously.

How long does a Rate Watch alert last?

You can choose how long your alert stays active, up to a maximum of 3 months.

If your selected rate is not reached within that time, the alert will expire automatically.

Will I get the exact rate I set in my alert?

No, the rate you set is a target rate only.

Rate Watch always reflects the latest Travel FX exchange rate for the product you selected.

Exchange rates move frequently and the current rate may not be available for long.
The rate available to you when placing your order will be the live rate at that time.

Are exchange rates guaranteed once I receive an alert?

No, exchange rates are not guaranteed.
A Rate Watch alert simply notifies you that your selected rate has been reached. It does not reserve or lock in that rate.

Exchange rates from Travel FX are only confirmed once your order has been completed.

How quickly will I be notified when my rate is reached?

We aim to notify you as quickly as possible when your selected rate is reached.

However, exchange rates move constantly and may change within seconds. Notifications should be used as a prompt to check the latest available rate rather than a guaranteed price.

Can I stop or delete a Rate Watch alert?

Yes, you can manage your alerts at any time.

To delete an alert:
  • Log in to your account
  • Go to the Rate Watch page
  • Select and delete your alert
What details do I need to provide?
To set up a Rate Watch alert, you’ll need to provide:
  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
Will my details be used for marketing?

No, your details will only be used to provide the Rate Watch service and send notifications relating to your alert.

They will not be used for unrelated marketing as part of this service.

What happens if I miss the alert?

Exchange rates can move quickly, and the rate may no longer be available if you miss the notification.

We recommend acting promptly if you receive an alert and want to secure a rate.

Why do exchange rates change so frequently?

Exchange rates are influenced by global markets, economic news and currency demand.

This means they can move multiple times throughout the day, which is why Rate Watch can help you time your purchase more effectively.
