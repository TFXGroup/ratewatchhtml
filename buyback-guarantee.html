ADDED PEACE OF MIND FOR YOUR TRAVEL MONEY

Ordering the right amount of currency for your trip isn't always an exact science. That's where our Currency Buyback Guarantee comes in - giving you the flexibility to return a portion of your unused currency at the same rate you bought it.

For a small fee, you can protect yourself from exchange rate changes and make the most of your travel money, even after your trip.

HOW IT WORKS

When placing your travel money order, simply add our Buyback Guarantee for £5 per currency. If you return with any leftover banknotes, you'll have 31 days from your dispatch date to sell up to 30% of your original order back to us - at the same exchange rate you paid.

  • No market fluctuations. No surprises.
  • £5 fee per currency
  • Valid for 31 days from the date your order is sent
  • Covers up to 30% of your original order value
  • For orders over £3,000, the maximum guaranteed buyback is £900

WHY CHOOSE THE BUYBACK GUARANTEE?

Exchange rates can shift quickly, and not always in your favour. By securing your rate on a portion of your order, you can avoid losses when exchanging any remaining currency.

For example, if you order £2,000 worth of Euros and return with £500, we'll buy it back at your original rate - not the rate on the day you return.

How to add Buyback Guarantee to your order.

You'll see the option to add the Buyback Guarantee during checkout. Just tick the box when placing your order online and it'll be included automatically.

How to use it when I return from my travels.

Once you're home, simply log in and follow our standard currency buyback process to send back your unused notes. If you're within the 31-day window and meet the terms, we'll apply your guaranteed rate with no extra steps.

Lock in your rate. Keep your options open.

The Travel FX Buyback Guarantee is a smart way to take the guesswork out of ordering currency. Add it to your next order and enjoy greater flexibility and control — wherever your travels take you.

  • Travellers with flexible or multi-destination plans.
